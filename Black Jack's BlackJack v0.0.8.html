How to Play

Setup

You're dealt 7 face-up cards. Tap a card to select it, then tap a hand slot to place it. Choose 6 cards to build 3 hands of 2 cards each — the 7th is discarded.

The Strategy

You can see all 7 cards. Choose wisely — which 6 you keep and where you place them is the whole game. The dealer's 6 cards are split randomly into 3 hands.

Playing

Once all cards are placed, your hands are revealed. Play Hand 1 vs Dealer Hand 1, then Hand 2 vs Hand 2, then Hand 3 vs Hand 3. Hit or Stand as normal blackjack. Dealer hits on 16 or below.

Winning

Best 2 hands out of 3 wins a match. Win 3 matches to win the series (best of 5). Your ELO rating goes up or down based on the final series result.

ELO

Start at 1200. Win a match and your ELO climbs. Lose and it drops. The stronger your rating, the less you gain from a win and the more you lose from a defeat.
